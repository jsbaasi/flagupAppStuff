Privacy Policy for FlagUp

1. Data We Collect: By using FlagUp, you consent to the collection and use of the following data:

    Discord User ID: To identify your unique account.

    Username & Display Name: To display your identity on leaderboards/results.

    Avatar/Profile Picture: To display your visual identity alongside scores.

    Game Data: Scores, guess history, and game statistics you submit.

2. How We Use Your Data: This data is used solely for the functionality of the game, specifically to maintain persistent leaderboards, track high scores, and display results within Discord. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Retention & Removal: We store your game data indefinitely to maintain global high scores. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by emailing jsbaasi@stormblessed.fr

4. Contact: For privacy concerns, please contact the developer at: jsbaasi@stormblessed.fr
